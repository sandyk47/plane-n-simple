About Us: What is Plane Simple?

PLNS is exploring benefits of introducing blockchain technology and smart contracts into a pilot project. Instead of selling seats (tickets) we are tokenizing aircraft.

To break this down, the owner of the aircraft receives money but does not yet issue a plane. Instead, it issues a token that can lease for that plane at a later date and time.

Once tokens are sold by the aircraft owner these can be traded, transferred or exchanged by their first owners many times before these will be exchanged for the full lease of aircraft.

Unique Selling Propositions

  • Transferability of the purchased aircraft to a new owner. Usual aircraft are not transferable or available for a pilot to freely lease at one's leisure. A token, which represents an aircraft to be redeemed, can be easily transferred to a new owner.
  • Tradability of a seat - usual aircraft are not tradable - they are issued/ bought by a particular company . tokens representing an aircraft lease at an arbitrary time can gain in value due to rising demand to fly.
  • Freedom and ability to both aircraft owner and leasee to capitalize on a high value asset.
